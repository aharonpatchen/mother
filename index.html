HAPPY MOTHER'S DAY, I LOVE YOU, THANKS FOR HAVING ME! and now a poem by Pat O'Reilly

God made a wonderful mother

A mother who never grows old;

He made her smile of the sunshine,

And He molded her heart of pure gold;
In her eye He placed bright shining stars,
In her cheeks fair roses you see; God made a wonderful mother, And He gave that dear mother to me.
